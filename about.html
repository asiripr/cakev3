Who we are...

Pramodaya's cake shop is located in Belihuloya village and it is growing rapidly among local and foreign tourists visiting the famous natural village. A group of university students started this business and through its development, today with the participation of experienced employees, it is able to provide customers with high-quality cake products.

Starting in early 2022, the cake shop has established trust among its customers by establishing a reputation. This website has been released with the aim of expanding this great business across the entire country by breaking geographical boundaries. Now you can order and receive our delicious and quality cake products online anywhere in Sri Lanka.
